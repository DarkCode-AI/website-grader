    What We Check (and Why It Matters)

    Our free website grader runs an 11-point health check on your site — the same checks that determine whether visitors become customers or leave for a competitor. Each check is weighted by its impact on lead capture and revenue.

    Live Chat / Chatbot (20 points) is the highest-weighted check because 62% of customers expect an immediate response. Without a chat widget, after-hours visitors — who make up 40% of all web traffic — have no way to reach you. That's leads walking out the door every night.

    Page Speed (15 points) and Mobile Responsiveness (15 points) together account for 30 points because Google confirms these directly affect both rankings and conversions. A 1-second delay in load time reduces conversions by 7%. Over 60% of searches happen on mobile — if your site breaks on phones, you're invisible to most customers.

    Online Booking (8 points) and Contact Form (10 points) are conversion essentials. Businesses with online booking capture 3x more appointments than those requiring a phone call. A visible phone number and contact form ensure no visitor leaves without a way to reach you.

    Reviews, Social Links, and Google Maps build the trust signals that turn browsers into buyers. 93% of consumers read online reviews before choosing a local business. If your site doesn't display social proof, customers assume the worst.

    How Your Score Is Calculated

    Your website receives a score out of 100 points across all 11 checks. Each check is weighted by its revenue impact — chatbot and speed matter more than social links because they directly affect whether visitors convert into paying customers.

    • Grade A (85-100): Your site is well-optimized for conversions. Minor tweaks only.
    • Grade B (70-84): Solid foundation but missing key conversion elements.
    • Grade C (50-69): Significant gaps — you're likely losing 20-40% of potential customers.
    • Grade D/F (below 50): Critical issues. Your website is actively driving customers to competitors.
